# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Listeners discovering BigZo for the first time, returning fans looking for the newest drop, and promoters or collaborators deciding whether to book or contact him. They are usually on mobile, coming from social, streaming, or direct links and want a fast path to music, video, events, and booking.

## Product Purpose
This site is BigZo's official artist hub. It should make the newest release feel immediate, reinforce the artist identity, drive streams and video views, and convert serious interest into feature bookings or event follow-through.

## Brand Personality
Bold, electric, and unapologetic. The tone should feel performance-first, high-energy, and confident without slipping into generic nightclub or template-heavy music branding.

## Anti-references
Do not make this feel like a corporate SaaS landing page, a soft pastel influencer template, or a generic dark-mode artist theme with weak hierarchy and interchangeable cards. Avoid timid layouts that bury the newest release.

## Design Principles
1. Lead with the latest drop so visitors immediately see what is new.
2. Keep paths to listen, watch, and book obvious with minimal friction.
3. Preserve a strong stage-energy identity through contrast, scale, and decisive placement.
4. Make mobile feel first-class without sacrificing the impact of the desktop presentation.
5. Favor clarity over ornament when a layout choice affects conversion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for core text and controls, keep navigation keyboard reachable, and ensure embedded media and motion-heavy sections still work cleanly on smaller screens. Reduced-motion-safe behavior should be preserved where animated elements are used.
